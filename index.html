Process

1. Meeting

We'll set up a time to go over your project's needs. During this phase we discuss the purpose of your website, your target audience, style, and budget.

2. Proposal

Shortly after, you will receive a formal proposal of the project that outlines the services that will be provided, project timeline, and price.

3. Coding

Next, I begin the coding and design process. Tests are run to ensure that your website operates smoothly across all devices. You will be updated periodically throughout the process.

4. Delivery

Once your project is complete your website will go live. Then you will receive all files, assets and domain registries over to you.
